Information about age grading on this site

Age grading for road and track races relies on data collected by Tom Bernhard and ARRS, then compiled by Alan Jones. Since race records constantly evolve, the accuracy of age grading depends on how recent the data is.

It's important to note that data releases may not always include all previously covered distances. For instance, the 2-mile track factor was present in the 2005 data but missing from the 2023 version. As a consequence, races held in 2023 and beyond won't have an age-graded score for that distance.

Current files used: Roads: 2025, Track 2023
